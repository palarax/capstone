# Picking models
Pick smaller models, as larger models can overfit training data. 
Important thing to consider is "Validation Loss". Keeping it small, yet closer to training loss is tricky and may need 
some regularization like DropOut or WeightDecay etc.
Minimumum of 20 images per class
